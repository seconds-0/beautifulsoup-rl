    CORSAIR AIR 5400 RS-R ARGB

    Audacious Cooling, Groundbreaking Design

    An innovative triple-chamber design, airflow ducts, 3x RS120-R ARGB fans, and a wraparound glass exterior merges stunning views with exceptional cooling.

    TRIPLE-CHAMBER DESIGN

    Minimize heat with separate chambers for the CPU, GPU/motherboard, and PSU, cables, and storage for exceptional thermals and an easy build process.

    DUAL AIRFLOW DUCTS

    Specialized ducts allow for continual upward airflow through the main chamber with cool air pulled from outside the chassis and channeled directly into the GPU.

    CPU AIO CHAMBER

    The CPU chamber is designed for all-in-one (AIO) liquid coolers to draw in fresh air from outside and immediately exhaust the heated air through a wide opening in the side for peak CPU performance.

    RS120-R ARGB FANS INCLUDED

    The bundled 3x RS120-R ARGB fans allow powerful, yet quiet intake airflow with unobstructed lighting for serious cooling and dazzling visuals.

    DUAL CURVED FRENCH DOOR GLASS PANELS

    Two glass panels connect in the corner of the chassis and open outwards with hinges that disappear into the case for a seamless look and easy access to your hardware.

    RAPIDROUTE 2.0

    Cable management is a breeze with an all-new pegboard motherboard tray and ratcheting cable ties that can be resized, rotated, and repositioned for a super-clean third chamber.

    ALL USB TYPE-C FRONT I/O

    The easily accessible top-mounted Front I/O offers a USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-C port and 2x USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type-C ports for blazing-fast data transfers.

    TAME YOUR TEMPERATURES

    There are 10x 120mm fan mounts to make sure every component has continuous cooling for peak performance.

    READY FOR UPGRADES

    The spacious interior easily fits oversized graphics cards and makes building in the AIR 5400 effortless.

    LOADED WITH FEATURES

    The AIR 5400 incorporates useful features to make building a PC easier.

    EASY CABLE MANAGEMENT

    Nylon brushes between the chambers allow for easy cable routing without any gaps to spoil the aesthetic.

    HIDE YOUR CABLES

    The AIR 5400 is compatible with reverse-connector motherboards from ASUS, MSI, and GIGABYTE for super-clean builds with no visible cables.

    SAG-FREE GRAPHICS CARDS

    A GPU Anti-sag Stabilization Arm keeps your GPU steady, so you never have to worry about damaging your PCIe slot.
